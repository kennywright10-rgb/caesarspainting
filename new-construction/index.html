New Construction Specialists

On Schedule. On Spec. Every Build.

New construction painting is a different discipline than repaint work. Builders need painters who understand construction sequencing, can coordinate with other trades, hit tight punch-list deadlines, and don't slow down the project. Caesar's Painting has worked with North Atlanta builders on residential and light commercial projects — and we show up ready to work.

Our new construction services cover everything from the initial primer coat after drywall to the final touch-up pass before the certificate of occupancy. We handle interior, exterior, trim, garage, and specialty coatings in one seamless package.
