More Than Paint — It's Protection

Your home's exterior is the first line of defense against Georgia weather — summer UV, humidity, pollen seasons, and occasional hard freezes. A proper exterior paint job isn't just cosmetic; it seals wood, prevents rot, and blocks moisture intrusion for years. Caesar's Painting uses weather-resistant exterior coatings and proven prep techniques to make sure your results last 8–12 years, not 2–3.

Our Exterior Process

01

Pressure Wash

We wash every surface to remove dirt, mildew, and chalk before a single drop of paint goes on.

02

Scrape & Repair

Failing paint is fully scraped. Rotted wood is replaced or filled. Caulk is applied at every joint and seam.

03

Prime

Bare wood, repairs, and stain-prone areas get a coat of oil or bonding primer before topcoats.

04

2-Coat Finish

We apply two full coats of premium exterior paint — Sherwin-Williams Emerald or Duration — for lasting protection.
