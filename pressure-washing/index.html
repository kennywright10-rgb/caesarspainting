Pressure Washing FAQs

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast away debris — ideal for concrete driveways, brick, and dense hardscapes. Soft washing uses lower water pressure combined with biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely clean siding, stucco, wood decks, and roofing without causing damage.

Once per year is recommended for most North Atlanta homes — ideally in early spring after pollen season. Homes with heavy tree coverage, north-facing siding, or heavy mildew growth may benefit from twice-yearly cleaning.

High-pressure washing can crack or force water behind vinyl siding. We always use soft wash technique on vinyl siding to clean it safely and effectively without the risk of damage or moisture infiltration.

Absolutely — and it's the right way to prep for exterior painting. We include a pressure wash in every exterior paint job. Booking them together saves money and ensures proper drying time before primer goes on.
