A Like-New Kitchen for 20% of the Cost

New kitchen cabinets average $15,000–$40,000 installed. Professional cabinet painting delivers the same visual transformation for $1,500–$5,000 — and with the right prep and coatings, results that hold up to daily use for 10+ years.

At Caesar's Painting, we don't cut corners on cabinet jobs. We degrease, sand, and prime every surface, then apply two topcoats with professional spray equipment in our controlled environment. No brush marks. No drips. Just smooth, durable results.

Our Cabinet Painting Process

01

Remove & Label

All doors and drawer fronts are removed, labeled, and transported to our spray area for a drip-free finish.

02

Degrease & Sand

Cabinet boxes are degreased in place. All surfaces are hand-sanded to 220-grit for maximum adhesion.

03

Prime

We apply a bonding primer designed for cabinetry — essential for adhesion on factory-finished surfaces.

04

Spray & Finish

Two coats of premium catalyzed enamel or cabinet-rated paint — sprayed for a furniture-grade finish.
