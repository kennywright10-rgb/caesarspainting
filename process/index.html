Step by Step

From First Call to Final Coat

Here's exactly what happens when you hire Caesar's Painting — from the moment you reach out to the day we hand you back your keys.

01

Free Estimate & Consultation

You reach out by phone or our contact form. We schedule a time to visit your home, walk through the project with you, discuss your goals and color ideas, and answer any questions you have. You receive a written, itemized estimate — no pressure, no obligation.

02

Color Selection & Scheduling

Once you accept the estimate, we finalize your color selections and schedule your start date. We'll bring swatches, review your existing finishes, and make sure your palette is right before we order any paint. You'll receive a written confirmation of your project dates.

03

Prep Work

This is where quality is made or lost. We cover floors and furniture, fill holes and cracks, sand rough surfaces, caulk gaps, prime bare spots, and mask every edge with precision tape. We never skip prep — it's what makes paint last and look right.

04

Painting

We apply premium paint with professional tools — rollers, brushes, and sprayers depending on the surface and project type. Two coats are standard. We cut clean lines on every edge — no bleed-over on trim, no paint on hardware, no roller marks on ceilings.

05

Cleanup

When the paint is dry, we remove all masking tape and drop cloths, put furniture back in place, clean up any dust or debris, and leave your home exactly as we found it — except with fresh, beautiful paint.

06

Final Walkthrough

We walk every room with you before we consider the job done. If you see anything that isn't right — a thin spot, a missed edge, anything — we fix it on the spot before we leave. We don't consider a project complete until you're fully satisfied.
