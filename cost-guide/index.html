What Affects Painting Cost

📐

Surface Area

More square footage means more paint and more time. Multi-story homes and large rooms cost more.

🔨

Prep Work

Drywall repairs, extensive caulking, stripping old paint, or significant scraping add to project cost.

🎨

Number of Colors

More colors mean more color changes, masking, and cleanup — adding to the time and cost.

🏠

Surface Condition

Homes with significant wear, texture issues, or stains require more prep and sometimes additional coats.
