FAQs

Questions We Hear All the Time

Honest answers to help you feel confident before you call.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Lawrenceville, GA? ⌄
House painting in Lawrenceville, GA typically costs between $1,500 and $6,500+ depending on square footage, number of stories, surface condition, and whether you need interior or exterior work. We offer 100% free, no-obligation estimates — call (404) 916-3260.
Do you offer free painting estimates in Gwinnett County? ⌄
Yes! We provide free estimates across all of Gwinnett and Forsyth Counties — including Lawrenceville, Buford, Snellville, Loganville, Cumming, Winder, and Alpharetta. Call or text (404) 916-3260.
Is Caesar's Painting licensed and insured in Georgia? ⌄
Absolutely. Caesar's Painting is fully licensed and insured in Georgia. We carry general liability and workers' compensation insurance to protect our clients and crew on every project.
How long does exterior house painting take? ⌄
Exterior painting typically takes 2–5 days depending on your home's size, weather conditions, and prep work required (such as wood repair or pressure washing). We'll give you a precise timeline during your free estimate.
Do you do pressure washing before painting? ⌄
Yes — proper surface preparation is everything. We include thorough pressure washing before all exterior projects, and we offer standalone pressure washing for driveways, fences, roofs, and more.
Do you repair wood before painting? ⌄
Yes. Rotten or damaged wood must be fixed before painting to prevent further damage. We provide wood repair including siding repair, trim repair, and fascia board replacement as part of our exterior packages.
