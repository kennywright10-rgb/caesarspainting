Common Questions About Painting in Georgia

Everything you need to know before hiring a painting contractor in Lawrenceville, Buford, Alpharetta, and surrounding cities.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Lawrenceville, GA? +
House painting in Lawrenceville, GA typically costs between $1,500 and $6,500+ depending on the size of your home, number of stories, surface condition, and whether you need interior or exterior work. Caesar's Painting provides free, detailed written estimates — call (404) 916-3260 to schedule yours.
Do you offer free painting estimates in Gwinnett County? +
Yes! Caesar's Painting offers 100% free, no-obligation estimates throughout all of Gwinnett and Forsyth Counties — including Lawrenceville, Buford, Snellville, Loganville, Cumming, Winder, and Alpharetta. Call or text (404) 916-3260 to schedule your free quote today.
Is Caesar's Painting licensed and insured in Georgia? +
Absolutely. Caesar's Painting is fully licensed and insured in the state of Georgia. We carry general liability and workers' compensation insurance on every project. You'll never have to worry about your home's protection when you hire us.
How long does exterior painting take? +
Most exterior painting projects take 2–5 days depending on the size of your home, weather conditions, and the amount of prep work required (pressure washing, wood repair, caulking). We'll give you a precise timeline during your free estimate.
Do you pressure wash before painting? +
Yes — proper surface prep is essential for a long-lasting paint job. We pressure wash all exterior surfaces before painting to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and peeling paint. We also offer standalone pressure washing services for driveways, fences, decks, and roofs.
Do you repair wood before painting? +
Yes! Rotten or damaged wood must be repaired before painting to prevent moisture intrusion and ensure the paint adheres properly. Caesar's Painting provides wood repair including siding repair, trim replacement, and fascia board repair as part of our exterior packages.
Do you paint commercial properties in Alpharetta and Cumming, GA? +
Yes! Caesar's Painting serves commercial clients throughout the greater Atlanta area, including Alpharetta, Cumming, Buford, and Lawrenceville. We work with offices, retail spaces, HOAs, apartment complexes, warehouses, and more — and we schedule around your business hours to minimize disruption.
