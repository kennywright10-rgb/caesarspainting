Pricing & Cost

Interior painting in North Atlanta ranges from $400–$900 per standard-sized room. A full house repaint (4–5 bedrooms plus common areas) typically runs $3,500–$8,000. Pricing depends on room size, ceiling height, number of colors, and prep work needed. See our full cost guide for detailed breakdowns.

Exterior painting typically runs $2,800–$7,000 for a standard home, depending on square footage, stories, siding type, and condition. Homes with significant prep work (rot, peeling, caulking) may be priced higher.

Our price includes all labor, premium paint and primer, surface prep, masking and drop cloths, cleanup, and a final walkthrough with touch-ups. We don't nickel-and-dime with add-on charges for basic prep items.

Paint & Materials

We use Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore exclusively. Both are premium-tier paints with excellent coverage, durability, and color accuracy. We do not use builder-grade or budget paints. You can also supply your own paint if you have a specific product you prefer — we'll let you know if we have any concerns with the product.

For most interior walls, we recommend eggshell (low sheen) — it's easy to clean and looks great. Flat/matte is good for ceilings and low-traffic areas. Satin works well in kitchens, bathrooms, and kids' rooms. Semi-gloss is best for trim, doors, and cabinets. We'll advise you on the best sheen for each surface during your consultation.

Yes — we need your color selections before we purchase paint. We offer a complimentary color consultation with every project to help you choose. If you want more in-depth color guidance, we offer standalone color consultations as well.

Licensing & Insurance

Yes. Caesar's Painting is fully licensed and carries both general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We are happy to provide certificates of insurance in any format your HOA, property manager, or building owner requires.

Our general liability insurance covers accidental damage caused by our crew during the project. In the unlikely event that something is damaged, we address it promptly and professionally. This is part of why we carry proper insurance coverage — it protects you as much as it protects us.
